Chocolate Brownies

Dark chocolate brownies with a fudgey, gooey centre

Follow this recipe for the perfect brownies. We recommend serving them whilst they're still a bit warm with a scoop of vanilla ice-cream!

Ingredients

  • Dark Chocolate - 200g
  • Butter - 140g
  • Granulated sugar - 225g
  • Large eggs - 2
  • Egg yolk - 1
  • Plain flour - 85g

Steps

  1. Melt the chocolate and butter together in a bain-mairie.
  2. Remove from the heat, add the sugar and mix in until well combined.
  3. Add in the eggs and egg yolk and then whisk the mixture.
  4. Once whisked, fold in the plain flour until it is just combined. (Don't over-mix it!)
  5. Pour the mixture into a baking tin and bake at 180c for 25 minutes.
  6. Remove and allow to cool
