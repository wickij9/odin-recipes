Blueberry Muffin Cookies

Blueberry muffin cookies with crumble topping

These soft chewy cookies have a hint of lemon and are packed full of blueberry jam.

This recipe will make around 45 cookies so feel free to divide as required for a smaller batch.

Ingredients

  • Plain flour - 1500g
  • Baking soda - 5 teaspoons
  • Melted butter - 900g
  • Salt - 2 teaspoons
  • Light brown soft sugar - 1150g
  • Granulated sugar - 250g
  • Large eggs - 6
  • Zest of lemon - 1
  • Bluberry jam - 400g

Steps

  1. Melt the butter in a pan or microwave and leave to cool for a bit.
  2. Combine the flour, baking soda, salt and lemon zest in a bowl and set aside.
  3. Once the butter has cooled, add it to a separate bowl with the sugars and combine.
  4. To this bowl add the eggs and mix well.
  5. Add the bowl of dry ingredients into the wet mixture and mix until a sticky dough is formed.
  6. For best results now let the dough chill in the fridge for a few hours.
  7. Roll the dough out into even balls. We recommend 80g each.
  8. In each ball form an opening with your thumb or the back of a teaspoon.
  9. Add a teaspoon of the blueberry jam into each of these. Make sure the cookie is jam side up when you add it to a baking tray.
  10. Add the dough balls to parchemtn lined baking trays, leaving enough space for them to spread out as they bake.
  11. Put the trays into a pre-heated oven at 180c and bake for 13 minutes.
  12. Remove and allow to cool.
